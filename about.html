About ResumeByte

ResumeByte is a resume and hiring platform designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers through data-driven matching.

What we are building

  • ATS-optimized resume generation with professional templates.
  • Role-specific keyword suggestions and resume quality scoring.
  • Employer-side candidate search with powerful filters.
  • Messaging system between candidates and recruiters.

Our mission

We aim to improve hiring outcomes by helping candidates showcase relevant skills and helping employers discover better-fit talent faster.
